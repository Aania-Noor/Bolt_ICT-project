Project Description:
BOLT is an innovative and user-friendly website designed to help users buy pets and pet accessories in a simple, safe, and convenient way. Our goal is to create an online platform where pet lovers can easily browse different types of pets, learn about their care, and purchase high-quality accessories, all in one place.
The website includes detailed product pages, and an easy-to-navigate interface. BOLT aims to make the pet-buying experience more accessible by providing clear information, attractive visuals, and a smooth shopping process.
Through this project, our team focused on enhancing user experience and improving website layout. BOLT reflects our collaborative effort to build a modern, functional, and engaging website.
